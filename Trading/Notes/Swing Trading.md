# The Ultimate Guide to Swing Trading for Beginners 2025
- Source: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UWKNLR4jOI0
- Time frames: Daily, Weekly, Monthly then 5 Minute and 15 Minute to execute orders
- Horizonal lines represent significant price levels people have historically agreed to sell or buy
- TA Indicators: Daily Short Moving Average (SMA), 8 Exponential Moving Average (EMA), Volume bar
- Assets below 200 D SMA are considered bullish from swing trading, while below are not.
- Rule do not swing trade stocks below 200 D SMA
- Breakout pattern (favorite strategy) is breaking through daily resistance
- A breakout is only strong if its tested daily top usually at least 2 or 3 times.
- Breakdown is breaking below key support
- Finding stocks to swing trade after 11 AM EST using stox.io for midday movers (large cap swings): change > 3%, MC > 1B, Price > $1, Volume > 500k
- Have a swing trading watchlist
- Must meet criteria: daily breakout, uptrend along 8 EMA, History of 8 EMA reclaim, above 200 SMA
- Ideal entry is as close to the 8 EMA as possible
- Sell position in quarters: 25% along breakout, 25% along breakout, and 50% to continue trailing along 8 EMA
- Stop is moved to 8 EMA
