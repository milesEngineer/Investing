DAILY LINKS

- **Fear and Greed Index**
  - Crypto Fear and Greed Index by TIA_Technologies uses [AlternativeMe](https://alternative.me/crypto/fear-and-greed-index/ "‌") for historical data.
  - Fear and Greed Index by [barnabygraham](https://www.tradingview.com/u/barnabygraham/ "‌")
- [**Short-Term Holder MVRV (Market Value to Realized Value) Ratio**](https://charts.checkonchain.com/btconchain/unrealised/mvrv_sth/mvrv_sth_light.html "‌")
  - The chart displays the average price of <155 day holders. If STH-MVRV > 1, short-term holders are in profit (market price exceeds their average purchase price).
  - **ACTION:** When the STH-MVRV is 2 standard deviations from 1, then buy or sell.
- [Short-Term Holder Spent Output Profit Ratio (STH-SOPR)](https://charts.checkonchain.com/btconchain/realised/sthsopr_indicator/sthsopr_indicator_light.html "‌")
  - The chart displays the profit or loss ratio of <155 day holders.
  - **ACTION**: When the STH-SOPR is 3 standard deviations from 1, then buy or sell.
- [MVRV Ratio](https://charts.checkonchain.com/btconchain/unrealised/mvrv_all/mvrv_all_light.html "‌")
  - Tracks the average unrealized profit or loss held by investors across the market.
    - ACTION: When the MVRV ratio is 3.7, then sell. Otherwise buy at <1.
- [Futures Funding Rates](https://charts.checkonchain.com/btconchain/premium/hodlers_dashboard/hodlers_dashboard_light.html "‌")
  - Funding rates can serve as a sentiment indicator for crypto trading decisions. The funding rate is the premium longs or shorts are willing to pay between the perpetual contract and the spot contract.
- [Net Unrealized Profit/Loss (NUPL)](https://chainexposed.com/NUPL.html "‌")
  - NUPL reflects the market’s overall unrealized profit or loss, providing insight into investor sentiment
- [RUP (Relative Unrealized Profit)](https://chainexposed.com/RelativeUnrealizedProfit.html "‌")
  - RUP provides insight into the profitability of Bitcoin holders and market sentiment
